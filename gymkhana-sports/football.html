⚽ Football

The World's Most Popular Sport

Football develops teamwork, fitness, quick decision-making, leadership and discipline. It is one of the most exciting and competitive outdoor sports played by students.

⚽ Football Rules

👥 Team Size

Each football team consists of 11 players including one goalkeeper. Substitutions are allowed according to tournament rules.

⏱ Match Duration

A standard match consists of two halves. The team scoring the highest number of goals wins the match.

⚽ Goal Scoring

A goal is scored when the entire ball crosses the goal line between the goalposts and under the crossbar.

🤝 Fair Play

Players must respect referees, teammates and opponents. Unsporting behaviour may lead to penalties or suspension.
