Precision, Focus and Accuracy

Experience the excitement of one of the most popular indoor tabletop sports that combines skill, strategy and concentration.

🎯 About Carrom

Carrom is a popular tabletop game played by striking pieces into corner pockets using a striker. It requires excellent hand-eye coordination, concentration and strategic thinking.

📜 Rules of Carrom

① Start of Game

The winner of the toss gets the first strike.

② Use the Striker

Players must use only the striker to hit coins.

③ Pocketing Coins

Players score by pocketing their assigned coins.

④ Queen Rule

The queen must be covered after pocketing.

⑤ Fouls

Wrong strikes and penalties count as fouls.

⑥ Fair Play

Players must maintain discipline and sportsmanship.
