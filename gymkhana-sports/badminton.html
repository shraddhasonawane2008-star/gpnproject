🏸 Badminton

Speed • Precision • Reflexes

Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport that develops agility, concentration, coordination and quick decision-making skills.

🏸 Badminton Rules

👥 Singles & Doubles

Matches can be played in singles (1 player) or doubles (2 players per team) format.

🎯 Scoring System

A match is generally played as best of 3 games. Each game is played up to 21 points.

🏸 Service Rules

The shuttlecock must be served diagonally into the opponent's service court.

🤝 Sportsmanship

Players must respect officials, opponents and follow fair play throughout the competition.
