🏃 Athletics

Speed • Endurance • Excellence

Athletics includes track and field events that test speed, strength, endurance and determination. It is one of the oldest and most respected sporting disciplines.

🏃 Athletics Rules

🏃 Track Events

Track events include sprints, middle-distance and long-distance races. Athletes compete to achieve the fastest time.

⏱ Timing

Athletes are ranked according to their performance time. The fastest competitor is declared the winner.

🚫 False Start

A participant who starts before the official signal may receive a warning or disqualification according to event rules.

🤝 Fair Competition

Athletes must follow event regulations, respect officials and maintain sportsmanship throughout the competition.
