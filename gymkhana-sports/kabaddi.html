🤼 Kabaddi

Strength • Strategy • Stamina

Kabaddi is one of India's most popular traditional sports. It develops physical fitness, quick decision-making, team coordination and mental strength.

🤼 Kabaddi Rules

👥 Team Size

Each team consists of 7 active players on the court. Substitutions are allowed according to tournament rules.

🏃 Raiding

A raider must enter the opponent's court, tag players and return safely while continuously chanting "Kabaddi".

🎯 Scoring

Points are awarded for successful raids and tackles. The team with the highest score wins the match.

🤝 Fair Play

Players must respect referees, opponents and tournament rules while maintaining discipline and sportsmanship.
