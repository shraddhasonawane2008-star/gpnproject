🏓 TABLE TENNIS

Speed, Reflexes and Precision

Experience the thrill of fast-paced rallies, lightning reflexes and competitive indoor action through the exciting sport of Table Tennis.

🏓 About Table Tennis

Table Tennis, also known as Ping Pong, is a fast-paced indoor racket sport played between two or four players. It requires excellent reflexes, hand-eye coordination, speed and concentration.

📜 Rules of Table Tennis

① Service Rule

The ball must bounce once on each side of the table during a serve.

② Scoring

A match is usually played up to 11 points.

③ Alternate Serves

Players change service after every two points.

④ Valid Return

The ball must be returned after one bounce.

⑤ Net Contact

If the ball touches the net during a rally and lands correctly, play continues.

⑥ Sportsmanship

Players must follow fair play and competition rules.
