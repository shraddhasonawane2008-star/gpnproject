🏏 Cricket

The Gentleman's Game

Cricket is one of the most popular outdoor sports. It develops teamwork, leadership, concentration, discipline and sportsmanship among students.

🏏 Cricket Rules

👥 Team Size

Each team consists of 11 players. Substitutes may be allowed according to tournament rules.

⏱ Match Format

College matches are generally played in limited-over format. The team scoring more runs wins.

🏏 Batting Rules

A batter is dismissed if bowled, caught, run out or given LBW according to cricket laws.

🤝 Fair Play

Players must respect umpires, opponents and follow sportsmanship throughout the match.
