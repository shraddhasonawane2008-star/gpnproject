♟ About Chess

Chess is one of the world's most popular strategic board games. It develops concentration, analytical thinking, patience and decision-making skills. The game is played between two players on a board of 64 squares and is widely recognized as a sport that challenges the mind.

📜 Rules of Chess

① Starting Position

Each player begins with 16 pieces placed in their standard positions.

② First Move

The player controlling the white pieces always makes the first move.

③ Piece Movement

Every chess piece has its own unique movement pattern.

④ Checkmate

The objective is to place the opponent's king in checkmate.

⑤ Touch Move Rule

If a player touches a piece intentionally, it must be moved if possible.

⑥ Fair Play

Players must follow sportsmanship and competition rules at all times.
