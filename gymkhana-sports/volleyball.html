🏐 Volleyball

Power • Precision • Teamwork

Volleyball is an exciting team sport that develops agility, coordination, communication and teamwork. It is one of the most energetic and fast-paced outdoor sports competitions.

🏐 Volleyball Rules

👥 Team Size

Each volleyball team consists of 6 players on the court. Substitutions are allowed according to tournament rules.

🏐 Maximum Touches

A team may touch the ball a maximum of three times before sending it over the net to the opposing side.

🎯 Scoring System

Points are scored when the ball lands on the opponent's court or when the opposing team commits a fault.

🤝 Sportsmanship

Players must respect officials and opponents while maintaining fair play and positive team spirit throughout the match.
